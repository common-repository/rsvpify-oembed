Add RSVPify to the available oEmbed providers to let you easily embed RSVPify event RSVP and event registration forms by just entering the event's URL.

== Description ==

Easily embed your event's online RSVP or event registration form on any page or post to allow guests to seamlessly RSVP from any WordPress-powered website. Guests can RSVP, register for your event, or purchase tickets without leaving your website.

== Installation ==

1. Download the RSVPify Event RSVP & Registration Embed plugin and extract it to your Wordpress's Plugins folder ({wordpress-folder}/wp-content/plugins)
2. Enter your Wordpress Administrator panel
3. Activate plugin under Plugins section
4. Go to post/page you want to embed your form
5. Paste or enter your RSVPify event URL's to your page or blog post (i.e. https://yourevent.rsvpify.com)
7. Save/Preview it
8. Voila! Your RSVPify form will be embedded on your page or post.

== Frequently asked questions ==

= How do I embed my RSVPify RSVP or registration form? =

Simply copy and paste your RSVPify URL into any page or post. Your form will be automatically embedded.

= What is RSVPify? =

RSVPify lets event hosts quickly and easily create custom online RSVP and event registration forms for any event. RSVPify offers a suite of features and tools to make event planning easy, from start to success.

You can accept RSVPs, process event registrations and sell tickets online with RSVPify.

RSVPify is 100% free for unlimited use for events selling tickets.

= Is RSVPify free? =

RSVPify is free for events with up to 100 RSVPs or invitees. Premium plans start at $19 per month. One-time, per-event plans are also available.
